Accommodation

In our cozy retreat, each en-suite room at Paternoster Lodge is a haven of coastal tranquility. Nestled by the Atlantic Ocean, our West Coast accommodations offer a perfect blend of comfort and scenic beauty. Wake up to breathtaking ocean views and indulge in the serene ambiance that defines our Lodge.

Restaurant

Savor the goodness at Paternoster Lodge Restaurant, where we mix West Coast flavors with a touch of culinary creativity. Nestled by the beautiful Atlantic Ocean, our dining experience is all about warmth and coziness.

Conference

Plan your business gatherings and events at Paternoster Lodge, where our conference facility brings a seaside touch to professionalism. Set by the Atlantic Ocean, our top-notch venue creates a perfect setting for productive meetings, conferences, and special events with a laid-back charm.

Events

Celebrate your special moments at Paternoster Lodge, where our event spaces are crafted to turn your occasions into lasting memories. By the Atlantic Ocean, our versatile venue offers a cozy backdrop for weddings, birthdays, and various special events, making each gathering truly special.
