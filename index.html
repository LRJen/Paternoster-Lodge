About Us

PaternosterLodge

Nestled in the heart of a charming fishing village, Paternoster Lodge offers a perfect blend of luxury and authentic coastal experiences. Our lodge has been designed to provide guests with ultimate comfort while preserving the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region.

Each aspect of our lodge reflects our commitment to excellence, from the carefully curated interiors to the locally-inspired cuisine served at our restaurant. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or a base to explore the stunning coastline, Paternoster Lodge provides an unforgettable experience for all our guests.

Our Location

Finding Us

Paternoster Lodge is situated in a picturesque fishing village, just a 2-hour drive from the nearest international airport. Our location offers the perfect balance of seclusion and accessibility.

The area is known for its pristine beaches, rich marine life, and colorful local culture. Within walking distance, you'll find charming restaurants, art galleries, and opportunities to experience authentic coastal life.
