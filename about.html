THE STORY ABOUT PASTERNOSTER LODGE

The story about Paternoster Lodge starts in a quaint West Coast town surrounded by pristine beaches, ocean views, and charming fishermen cottages. Paternoster invites you to immerse yourself in the unique culture and breathtaking sunsets of this coastal gem. Home to a variety of sea life, wildlife, and over 200 bird species. Paternoster offers a tranquil escape. In spring, witness the enchanting bloom of wildflowers, transforming the region into a floral paradise. Discover the Cape Columbine Nature Reserve’s 263ha of coastal beauty, featuring the last manually controlled lighthouse in South Africa—the Cape Columbine Lighthouse—perched on Castle Rock since 1936.
